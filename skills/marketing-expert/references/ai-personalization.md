# AI-Driven Hyper-Personalization (2026)

## From Segmentation to 1:1
Traditional marketing uses broad segments (e.g., "Males 25-34"). 2026 marketing uses real-time, individual data.

---

### 1. Intent Prediction
AI models analyze mouse movements, scroll depth, and click patterns to predict if a user is "browsing," "comparing," or "ready to buy."

- **Orchestration**: If intent = "ready to buy", trigger a "One-Click Checkout" overlay.
- **Orchestration**: If intent = "comparing", show a "Competitor Comparison" chart.

---

### 2. Dynamic Pathing
The website layout reconfigures itself based on the user's past interactions.

- **Example**: A developer visiting a SaaS site sees "Documentation" and "API Keys" first. A CEO sees "Case Studies" and "ROI Calculator."

---

### 3. LLM-Integrated Purchasing
AI agents (like the ones in this core) now act as intermediaries. 
- **Pattern**: Your site must provide "AI-Readable Data" (structured JSON/Schema.org) so that a user's personal agent can "research and buy" without the human ever visiting the site.

---

### 4. Predictive Retention
AI identifies "churn signals" (decreased login frequency, searching for "cancellation") and automatically triggers a personalized "Win-back" offer before the user leaves.

---
